Usage

$ kano
+----+---------------------------------+------+----------+-------+-------------+------------------+
| ID | Title                           | Type | Progress | Total | Your Rating | Community Rating |

| 0  | Brave Witches                   | TV   | 2        | 12    |             | 3.58             |
| 1  | Love Live! Sunshine!!           | TV   | 12       | 13    |             | 3.79             |

> search girlish
